## Protocol

Spec-allowed simplification of full nested CV: Optuna optimized a single **inner 3-fold** grouped CV (group=`patient_nbr`, seed 42) over the whole 80% train; the chosen params were scored across the **5 frozen outer folds** (`make_cv_folds`) for the reported tuned CV. Inner partition ≠ outer partition, but both cover the same 80% rows, so the outer CV is mildly optimistic — the holdout row is the unbiased number. The 20% holdout was scored exactly once.

## `first_encounter_only` sensitivity

Re-running the tuned params' 5-fold outer CV on the first-encounter-only cohort (69,990 rows) gives AUROC 0.657 (AUPRC 0.181), a shift of -0.019 vs the all-encounters tuned CV (0.676). Material shift — the repeat-encounter structure warrants discussion. No new tuning was done for this check.
